How the maths answers your questions.
• "£X/day → how much DAU?" per market: installs = spend ÷ CPI (mild saturation); DAU = retention-weighted accumulation of every cohort. Each sustained 1 install/day ≈ 5–6 DAU (T3) up to ≈ 8–9 DAU (Tier-1 iOS) at steady state.
• "For how long?" the curve climbs and plateaus; "days→80%" shows how fast it gets most of the way (same ramp shape across markets).
• "How much to maintain?" hold-flat installs = DAU ÷ steady-per-install, minus organic, priced at your mix's blended CPI.
• "Does spending more upmarket help ARPDAU?" yes — blended ARPDAU is DAU-weighted, so it rises as premium markets take a bigger DAU share; see the breakdown & allocation-sensitivity tables.
• "What if I spend a burst then stop?" the Campaign P&L integrates every cohort's revenue over 12 months, including the long retention tail after spend ends — so cumulative profit keeps rising at zero further cost. This is why lifetime profitability exceeds the steady-state monthly snapshot.
