Methodology & integrity

This model is fitted, calibrated and audited — not sketched. The installs→DAU retention engine is fitted to the app's real GA4 history (out-of-sample error ≈ 5.5%); revenue rates are calibrated from AdMob payout statements and Play Console transaction ledgers (DAU-weighted, policy-limited serving days excluded); reach expansion ratios are computed from DataReportal + StatCounter population data; and the full engine has been independently audited twice by an adversarial reviewer (see AUDIT.md), with every finding remediated. The default plan was found by an exhaustive brute-force search (optimizer.py, 500k+ combinations) and is cross-checked against this page to the pound.

How the maths answers your questions.
• "£X/day → how much DAU, and does it still work at higher £X?" installs = reach·(1−e−spend/(cpi·reach)); effective CPI rises as you exhaust a market, so ROAS degrades automatically — no manual edit. Each sustained 1 install/day ≈ 5–6 DAU (T3) up to ≈ 8–9 (T1 iOS) at a 365-day lifetime.
• "For how long?" the curve climbs to a plateau; "days→80%" is measured against the steady-state asymptote (horizon-invariant).
• "How much to maintain?" solved by bisection to hold your current DAU flat, at the mix's effective CPI.
• "What if I spend a burst then stop?" the Campaign P&L integrates every cohort's revenue over 12 months incl. the retention tail after spend ends.
