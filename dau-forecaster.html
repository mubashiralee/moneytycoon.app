How the maths answers your questions.
• "£X/day → how much DAU?" installs = spend ÷ CPI (with mild saturation), then DAU = retention-weighted accumulation of every cohort. Each sustained 1 install/day ≈ 5–6 DAU at steady state (1-yr horizon).
• "For how long?" the curve climbs and plateaus; "days→80%" shows how fast it gets most of the way.
• "How much to maintain?" hold-flat installs = DAU ÷ 5.3, minus your organic, converted to £ via CPI.
