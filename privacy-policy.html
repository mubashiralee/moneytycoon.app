Information We Collect

Information Stored Locally on Your Device

Money Tycoon is designed with your privacy in mind. All game data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers. This includes:

  • Player name and game progress
  • In-game financial data (virtual cash, businesses, investments)
  • Game settings and preferences
  • Achievement progress
  • Tutorial completion status

Information Collected Automatically

When you use our App, we may automatically collect certain information, including:

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: App feature usage, session duration, and crash reports
  • Purchase Information: Records of in-app purchases made through Google Play
  • Advertising Identifiers: Google Advertising ID (AAID) and similar identifiers used by Google AdMob to serve ads, measure ad performance, and provide frequency capping. You can reset or opt out of personalized ads via your device's advertising settings.

Information from Third-Party Services

Google Play Billing

When you make in-app purchases, the transaction is processed by Google Play. We receive confirmation of your purchase but do not have access to your payment details (credit card numbers, bank account information, etc.). Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for information on how Google handles your payment data.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the App
  • Process and fulfill in-app purchases
  • Send you technical notices and support messages
  • Respond to your comments and questions
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve user experience
  • Detect, prevent, and address technical issues

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

All game progress and player data is stored locally on your device using encrypted local storage. This game data is:

  • Not transmitted to our servers
  • Not shared with third parties for marketing
  • Deleted when you uninstall the App or clear App data

Note: This local-only storage rule applies to your game save data. Advertising identifiers, ad interaction data, and purchase records described above are processed by Google AdMob and Google Play in accordance with their respective privacy policies.

Your Rights and Choices

Data Deletion

Since all game data is stored locally on your device, you can delete all your data at any time by:

  • Uninstalling the App
  • Clearing the App's data through your device settings
  • Using the "Reset Game" feature within the App

Opt-Out of Analytics and Personalized Ads

You have several options to limit data collection:

  • Personalized Ads: On Android, go to Settings → Google → Ads and toggle "Opt out of Ads Personalization", or use the "Delete advertising ID" option.
  • GDPR Consent: Users in the EEA, UK, and Switzerland can revisit their consent choices via the App's privacy settings, which re-launches the consent form.
  • Subscription: Upgrading to our premium Gold or Platinum subscription tier removes all advertisements from the App entirely.
  • In-App Settings: You can also limit non-essential data collection via the App's Settings screen.

In-App Purchases

Money Tycoon offers optional in-app purchases including:

  • Cash packs (virtual in-game currency)
  • Speed boost consumables
  • Premium subscription tiers (Dividend Society)

All purchases are processed securely through Google Play. We do not store or have access to your payment information.

  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent for personalized advertising at any time via the App's privacy settings.

Legal Basis: We rely on your consent for personalized advertising (collected via Google's User Messaging Platform), the performance of a contract for in-app purchases, and legitimate interests for anonymous analytics and crash reporting.
